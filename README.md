1.  Project Overview & Purpose 

Bright Future Youth Tutoring Centre is an educational support organisation established to serve high school learners aged 13–18 (Grades 8–12) in Bizana and surrounding areas of the Eastern Cape. It was founded to address a clear community gap: the lack of safe, structured, and accessible academic support outside regular school hours. Many learners struggle with core subjects such as Mathematics, English, Life Sciences, and Computer Studies but have nowhere to go for extra help. 

Rationale for Selection 

Education is central to individual growth and community development. I chose this organisation because its core challenge is low visibility — despite delivering valuable services, many parents and learners never discover the centre exists. A dedicated website solves this by acting as a permanent, accessible information hub available 24/7. 

Project Objectives 

Increase visibility and searchability for local tutoring-related searches 

Present clear, complete details regarding subjects, schedules, and registration 

Simplify enquiries and contact through multiple channels 

Build professional credibility and trust with parents, schools, and guardians 

Provide an easy-to-update platform for announcements and news updates 

Mission Statement 

"To support students by providing quality tutoring and learning resources that help them succeed academically." 

Vision Statement 

"To create a future where every learner has access to the support and knowledge they need to reach their full potential."

8.  Conclusion  

Conclusion 

This design and development plan directly addresses the core challenge of Bright Future Youth Tutoring Centre: limited visibility and lack of organised online information. By combining clear educational messaging, mobile-friendly performance, and design choices focused on conversion, the website will transform from a simple brochure into a true community hub. 

When launched, it will ensure that learners and parents in Bizana and surrounding Eastern Cape areas can easily find the academic help they need — exactly aligning with the organisation's mission: every learner has the support required to reach their full potential
